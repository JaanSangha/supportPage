Introduction

Welcome to Trailblazer ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use our App, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, and password
  • Profile Information: Bio, avatar image, and any other profile details you choose to provide
  • User-Generated Content: Trip information (title, description, photos), point-of-interest (POI) data, ratings, reviews, check-ins, and comments

Location Information

  • GPS Location: We collect your precise location when you use location-based features to:
    • Show your position on the map
    • Help you plan trips and routes
    • Enable location-based POI discovery
    • Provide location-based recommendations

Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used and time spent

Media Access

  • Camera Access: To allow you to take photos for your trips and POI check-ins
  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select and upload existing photos for your trips and POI check-ins

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide Core Services

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Enable trip planning and sharing functionality
  • Provide mapping and navigation services
  • Allow POI discovery and community features

2. Enhance User Experience

  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user interface
  • Provide customer support

3. Community Features

  • Enable sharing of trips and POIs with other users
  • Facilitate ratings and reviews of locations
  • Support social features like check-ins and comments

4. Technical Operations

  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Analyze app usage to improve performance
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to other users:

  • Your profile name and avatar
  • Public Trips you create (title, description, photos, route information)
  • POIs you create or edit
  • Check-ins and associated photos

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication service provider
  • Mapbox: Our mapping and location services provider
  • Expo: Our app development platform

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect the safety of our users

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

Data Storage and Retention

  • Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase
  • We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your personal data
  • Some information may be retained for legal or technical reasons even after account deletion

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control

  • View and update your profile information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control the privacy settings of your content

Location Services

  • You can disable location services in your device settings
  • Note that disabling location services will limit certain app features

Media Access

  • You can revoke camera and photo library access in your device settings
  • This will prevent you from adding photos to trips and check-ins

Communication Preferences

  • You can opt out of promotional communications
  • Account-related communications are essential and cannot be opted out of
